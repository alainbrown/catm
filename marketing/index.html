Read anything out loud. Locally.

catm is a Chrome extension that turns any selected text into natural-sounding audio. The Kokoro 82M model runs entirely in your browser on WebGPU — no servers, no accounts, no uploads. Listen to articles, papers, and PDFs while you keep working.
