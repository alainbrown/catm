What's different from v4

  • Settings dissolved. No gear. Voice lives as a chip in the editor head; model lives as a full-width tier strip above the stage.
  • Tier strip. One card per tier. The installed tier shows ✕ to remove (with confirm). Others show ↓ Download or Coming soon. Delete-model lives next to the thing it deletes.
  • Voice popover. Click the voice chip — list of voices, each with a small ▶ preview.
  • Library footer. A single Clear library danger action. (Removing a tier is on the tier strip.)
  • Editor stays focused. Only the voice chip sits under the title; the model picker no longer competes for room.
  • About → colophon. The "About" panel from v4 is gone; the footer colophon carries provenance.
